Our Projects / Clients

AutoMio

Automio.ch is a modern platform we developed using Node.js and React.js technologies, delivering a fast and responsive user experience. The platform utilizes MongoDB as a database for efficient data management, while Cloudinary provides secure and scalable multimedia storage. We implemented SendGrid for reliable email communication with users. The entire system is hosted on Heroku, ensuring easy management and scalability of the application. Our team was responsible for the entire development process, from the initial concept to final implementation and maintenance.

RecycleCap

RecycleCap.com is an innovative platform we developed to enhance the recycling of plastic caps. Our team used advanced web technologies, including Node.js and React.js, to create an interactive and user-friendly application that allows users to easily participate in the collection and recycling process. The platform features functionalities for tracking collected caps, user communication, and promoting environmental awareness. We utilized MongoDB for data management, Cloudinary for secure storage, and SendGrid for email communication. The entire system is hosted on Render, ensuring stability, scalability, and efficiency, contributing to global environmental preservation efforts.

Bacchus Drinks

Bacchus Drinks is a platform we developed using the Java programming language for the backend and React for the frontend, providing a robust and reliable online shopping experience. The platform utilizes a PostgreSQL database for efficient management of user and product data, while SendGrid is integrated for email communication, ensuring reliable interaction with users. For invoice payments, we implemented Stripe, enabling users to securely and easily process transactions. Bacchus Drinks functions as an online store where users can register and purchase wines and other products offered. Our team was responsible for the complete development of this sophisticated e-commerce platform.

Mini Brocki

Minibrocki is a platform we developed using the Java programming language for the backend and React for the frontend, providing users with a simple way to buy and advertise old items, including antiques. The platform uses a PostgreSQL database for efficient management of user and product data. SendGrid is integrated for email communication, ensuring reliable interaction with users, while Stripe enables secure and easy payment processing. Minibrocki is the perfect destination for those looking to sell or purchase unique vintage items. Our team was responsible for the complete development of this specialized e-commerce platform.

NegotiateWind

NegotiateWind helps you create customised football contracts. It allows you to have full transparency over your agreements in one place, so no need to scroll through messages and try to understand what and with whom you agreed. You don't need to speak the same language as your contact, just select the language you want to negotiate with and your client will be able you choose the language he/she is comfortable with. All you need to do is add a person you want to negotiate terms with, fill in preconfigured fields and send for an agreement. It is also possible to add contacts (spectators) who should be aware of the agreement. Once the counterparty accepts, rejects or suggests changes to your proposal, you will get notifications. After both parties accept the offer, the contract will be finished successfully and the final PDF document with the agreement will be generated.
